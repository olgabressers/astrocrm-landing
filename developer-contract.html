Background & Scope of Work

Client is developing a SaaS product called AstroCRM — a client relationship management application for coaches, astrologers, therapists and other practitioners.

Developer is engaged on a freelance, on-demand basis to assist with software development tasks as defined in individual task briefs or statements of work agreed between the Parties. This Agreement governs all work performed under any such engagement.

3

Non-Disclosure & Confidentiality

3.1 Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by Client to Developer in connection with the work, including but not limited to:

  • Source code, architecture, algorithms and technical documentation
  • Business strategy, pricing, product roadmap and market plans
  • Customer data, user lists and any personal data
  • Credentials, API keys, database access and login details
  • Design files, wireframes and user interface concepts
  • Any information marked as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential

3.2 Obligations

Developer agrees to:

  1. Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
  2. Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent of Client
  3. Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of completing assigned tasks for Client
  4. Not use Confidential Information for Developer's own benefit or for the benefit of any third party
  5. Immediately notify Client upon becoming aware of any actual or suspected breach of this Agreement

3.3 Duration

The confidentiality obligations in this clause survive termination of this Agreement and remain in force for a period of 5 (five) years from the Effective Date, and indefinitely with respect to trade secrets.

3.4 Exceptions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Developer; (b) was already known to Developer before disclosure; (c) is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided Developer gives Client prompt written notice.

4

Intellectual Property Assignment

4.1 Work Made for Hire

All work product created by Developer in the course of performing services for Client — including but not limited to source code, scripts, databases, designs, documentation and any other deliverables — is considered work made for hire and is the sole and exclusive property of Client from the moment of creation.

4.2 Assignment

To the extent any work product does not qualify as work made for hire under applicable law, Developer hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers and conveys to Client all rights, title and interest in and to such work product, including all intellectual property rights, worldwide, in perpetuity, free of charge.

4.3 Moral Rights

Developer waives any moral rights or equivalent rights in the work product to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law.

4.4 Pre-existing Materials

If Developer incorporates any pre-existing tools, libraries or code (including open-source components) into the deliverables, Developer shall: (a) disclose this to Client in advance; (b) ensure such materials are properly licensed for use in a commercial product; (c) not incorporate any materials that would require Client to open-source the AstroCRM product or its derivatives.

4.5 Assistance

Developer agrees to sign any additional documents and take any actions reasonably requested by Client to register, perfect or enforce the intellectual property rights assigned herein.

5

Non-Compete & Non-Solicitation

5.1 Non-Compete

During the term of engagement and for a period of 12 (twelve) months after the last date of work performed under this Agreement, Developer agrees not to:

  1. Develop, build, or contribute to any software product that is directly competitive with AstroCRM — specifically a CRM application targeting coaches, astrologers, therapists or similar practitioners
  2. Use Confidential Information or knowledge gained through this engagement to develop a competing product

5.2 Non-Solicitation

For a period of 12 months after the last date of work, Developer agrees not to directly solicit Client's customers or business partners with whom Developer became acquainted through this engagement.

5.3 Reasonableness

Developer acknowledges that the restrictions in this clause are reasonable and necessary to protect Client's legitimate business interests, and do not prevent Developer from working as a software developer generally.

6

Payment & Working Arrangements

6.1 Compensation

Developer shall be compensated as agreed in individual task briefs or statements of work. No work shall be commenced without written agreement on scope and payment. Client's payment obligation arises upon delivery and acceptance of the agreed deliverable.

6.2 Milestone-Based Payment

Unless otherwise agreed, payment shall be made per completed and accepted milestone. Client reserves the right to withhold payment for deliverables that do not meet the agreed specification, pending correction by Developer.

6.3 Independent Contractor

Developer is an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment relationship, partnership or joint venture. Developer is solely responsible for their own taxes, social contributions and insurance in their country of residence.

6.4 Access & Security

Client will provide access only to the specific systems and code required for each task. Developer agrees not to access, copy or retain any systems beyond what is necessary for the assigned work. Upon completion or termination, Developer shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and confirm this in writing.

7

Governing Law & Disputes

7.1 Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Netherlands, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

7.2 Jurisdiction

Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent courts in the Netherlands.

7.3 Remedies

Developer acknowledges that any breach of Sections 3 (Confidentiality) or 4 (IP Assignment) may cause irreparable harm to Client for which monetary damages may be inadequate. Client shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief or other equitable remedies in addition to any other rights available at law.

7.4 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

💡 Before you send this contract

  • For Russian/Belarusian/Ukrainian developers: Dutch law governs, but enforcement requires them to be in the EU or have assets here — the contract still deters most people

  • Add a specific task brief as an Annex for each new piece of work — keeps scope clear and avoids disputes
  • Never share production credentials or the database — give developers only what they need for the specific task
