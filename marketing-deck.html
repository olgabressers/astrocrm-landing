How to use
Each deck is a single shareable URL. Send the link directly to the person — by email, WhatsApp, or DM. The pages are delisted (noindex, not in sitemap, blocked in robots.txt) — they exist on the site but are invisible to search engines and unlinked from public navigation. To add a new audience deck: copy the structure, swap the six nodes for that niche's pain points, save in landing/, add the path to robots.txt and to this page.
