Goal: find best tool to create YouTube + Instagram videos with automatic translation into Russian, English, Dutch. Use case: screen recording of CRM + voiceover, auto-dubbed into 3 languages. Solo non-technical founder.

Recommendation

Best overall
HeyGen
Free tier · $29/mo Creator plan
Screen recording + voiceover → auto-translated and dubbed in RU/EN/NL. All in one platform. Most intuitive for non-technical users. 70+ languages.
Budget option
CapCut + ElevenLabs
CapCut free · ElevenLabs $5/mo
CapCut edits video, ElevenLabs generates natural-sounding voices in RU/EN/NL. More manual work — but $5/month total. Good starting point.
Runner-up
VEED.io
Free (watermark) · $25/mo Plus
Browser-based, no install. Excellent subtitle auto-translation. Best for quick edits. Dubbing is partial — subtitles only, not voice replacement.

Recommended Workflow — Screen Recording → 3-Language Video

1
Claude writes the script in Russian — word for word, what to say on screen. Also writes SEO title, description, tags for YouTube.
2
You record screen + voiceover in Russian — just follow the script. Any screen recorder works (QuickTime on Mac is free). No editing needed.
3
Upload to HeyGen → Video Translation feature → select English + Dutch → HeyGen generates dubbed versions automatically. Your voice, translated.
4
Upload 3 versions to YouTube — Russian on main channel, English and Dutch as separate videos or with subtitle tracks. One recording → 3 markets.
5
For Instagram Reels — cut the 60-second highlight in CapCut (free), add auto-captions in Russian. Post on Instagram. Claude provides the caption text.
